# Privacy Policy — RF Explorer (Android)

_Last updated: 2026-06-15_

This app connects to an RF Explorer spectrum analyzer over USB and displays the RF
measurements it streams. It is a local instrument tool.

## Data collection

**This app collects no personal data.** It does not contain analytics, advertising,
crash reporting, or any networking code. Nothing is transmitted off the device.

## Data the app handles locally

- **Spectrum measurements** read from the connected RF Explorer over USB.
- **CSV exports** you explicitly create, written to the app's own external files
  directory on your device. These files stay on the device unless you share them
  yourself. The app never uploads them.

## Permissions

- **USB host access** — required to communicate with the RF Explorer hardware. Granted
  per-device by the Android system permission dialog.

The app requests no internet permission.
